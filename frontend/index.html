FinSwarm Guide

Welcome to FinSwarm

FinSwarm uses 14 specialized AI agents working together to analyze market events, debate outcomes, and project stock price valuations.

Input Stock News

Type a news headline, paste an article, or attach files. You can also use the Voice Button to record and transcribe your inputs verbally.

Select Simulation Mode

Choose between two modes:

  • Live Agent Debate: Watch the agents interact, update sentiments, and challenge each other.
  • Silent Consensus: Run the simulation instantly and jump straight to the consensus results while browsing dynamic company facts in the background.

Valuation & Future Projection

Analyze 12-month historical stock prices and see the 6-month projected future price path generated dynamically from the debate consensus.
